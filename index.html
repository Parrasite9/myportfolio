Beauty Salon

Studio No. 1

Welcome to Studio No. 1, a vibrant beauty salon home to a diverse team of over a dozen independent beauty professionals. Our biggest challenge? Ensuring our clients can easily connect with us, despite each team member having their own booking links. To address this, we embarked on a project to unify our presence and make it easier for clients to find and book appointments with any team member. Built with React and deployed on the reliable AWS platform using Route 53, CloudFront, S3, and ACM, our new system seamlessly brings together our individual talents under one virtual roof. Experience the convenience of Studio No. 1 and discover a team dedicated to enhancing your beauty and wellness journey.
